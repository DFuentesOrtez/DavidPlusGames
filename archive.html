MY GAMES OF 2024

Dark Souls 2: Scholar of the First Sin

Start Date: January 12th, 2024

End Date: January 19th, 2024

Rating: 8.5

Time: 40.1(77.3) Hours

Completion: Main Story

Thoughts:

*Just for context. I played about 37 hours in my first playthrough but my save was deleted so I restarted with a different build*

I underestimated the length of this game. There are way more bosses than the first game. Though that can provide a variety in combat, many bosses were easy and boring. The common enemies were more difficult than some bosses.

The most annoying part of the game was adventuring, some places were cool but irritating to get through, or just long.

Besides those dislikes. The game graphically was similar to the first, though I would say I loved some of the backgrounds in many parts of the map where you had a chance to overlook the land.

The combat was okay, nothing too different. Though I felt like some of the hit detection was way off. Also the camera felt awful, absolutely hated it but found a fix which made it a lot better.

Overall I decently enjoyed the game but at a certain point I just wanted to get it over with.

Lies of P

Start Date: January 4th, 2024

End Date: January 10th, 2024

Rating: 9.4

Time: 43.5 Hours

Completion: Main Story

Thoughts:

This game is amazing.

The art style and world is great. Playing as pinnochio in a world full of evil puppets. The story is easy to follow, it's clear what you are there to do as you progress through the game. The world as a whole was dark and scary in places that seem like they used to be so bright and joyful.

The system in the game was just as you would expect from a soulslike game so there's not much to explain there. But one thing is that the game is more linear than a typical souls game, the game will always lead you to where you should go and you do bosses in a certain order.

The combat and movement were great and smooth, everything felt quick, responsive, and especially satisfying. I kind of wish there was more variety in the classes and weapons but nonetheless I enjoyed my dexterity run.

This game is amazing and does justice to the souls genre.

Death's Door

Start Date: January 1st, 2024

End Date: January 4th, 2024

Rating: 9.0

Time: 8.7 Hours

Completion: Main Story

Thoughts:

This game's art style is cute and simple. I did not think I would get as invested in this game as I did.

At first I didn't think too much of it but was still enjoying it. But the dialogue got me slightly more invested over time, a lot of the time it was funny but at times it got deep. The story was sad and had me attached.

The "levels" were pretty creative, they weren't too difficult yet they were enjoyable. The combat was simple with it being a hack n slash type of game. And there wasn't much variety in weapons. The boss fights were pretty fun and had good variety in design and attacks.

Overall I enjoyed the game, my favorite part being the dialogue.

Tunic

Start Date: December 22nd, 2023

End Date: January 1st, 2024

Rating: 8.8

Time: 18.0 Hours

Completion: Main Story

Thoughts:

The art style in this game is 10/10. So cute and sick at the same time. One of my favorite parts.

The combat in this game was simple yet fun, the mechanics introduced over time made the combat more fun over time, letting you be more agile and try out different weapons.

The world was fun to explore, I loved the secret passages all around, letting you take some shortcuts once you get through areas to help you traverse more quickly.

Some aspects were definitely souls inspired, like the fox statues acting as bonfires which also restored your flasks. Souls-like components are usually a plus for me.

I really enjoyed playing through this game.

MY GAMES OF 2023

Luigi's Mansion 3

Start Date: Unknown

End Date: December 10th, 2023

Rating: 7.9

Time: 15.0 Hours

Completion: Main Story

Thoughts:

I have not played Luigi's Mansion 1 or 2 (I hear 1 is the best), so this is my first.

This game is so cute and funny. Graphically one of the best games I have played so far on the Switch.

Super Mario Wonder

Start Date: October 28th, 2023

End Date: December 6th, 2023

Rating: 9.0

Time: 25.0 Hours

Completion: 100%

Thoughts:

As with most Mario games, this game had amazing sound and graphics.

I loved the new "mechanic" of the wonder seed which can add even more to a level or change the level completely. This provides a fun twist to each level instead of a more generic level where you know what to expect from the start.

One of my favorite parts of the game was definitely the badge challenges, levels based around mastering your use of each badge mechanic. And the special world was my absolute favorite part. I looked forward to progressing in the game just to get to these levels. All the levels in this world were rated of the hardest difficulty, according to the game itself. They provided a fun challenge.

I really enjoyed playing through this game and 100%-ing it.

Portal

Start Date: December 4th, 2023

End Date: December 4th, 2023

Rating: 8.7

Time: 6.0 Hours

Completion: Main Story

Thoughts:

This is my second experience with Portal. The first being with the Portal 2 Coop.

The design and strategy of these levels still hold up in my eyes, regardless of the game now being 16 years old. Often I was stumped, wondering how I was supposed to continue.

Sometimes I was even scared playing. The occasional gaps in the walls revealing hidden rooms, messages, and artwork provided eerie feelings as I snooped around trying to find any secrets.

My favorite part had to be the final level, navigating through the facility, using the skills learned to power through.

Just glad the cake was not a lie.

Super Mario Bros

Start Date: October 23rd, 2023

End Date: October 23rd, 2023

Rating: 8.4

Time: 3.0 Hours

Completion: Main Story

Thoughts:

I can not imagine being a kid in 1985 trying to play this game and beat it. This game can be annoyingly difficult and the fact that you have to beat the game without losing your limited amount of lives is insane (though there is a secret button you can play to have checkpoints).

Besides that though, this game was quite simple and fun. And I could not stop playing until I beat the game. I spent lots of time trying to do it all in one run. There was a certain point though where I did end up using that secret checkpoint key in order to go back to the last world after wiping out.

I think I could come back to this game sometime and atttempt to do it all in one go. This very old game was solid and a fun few hours.

Dark Souls Remastered

Start Date: July 2nd, 2023

End Date: July 13th, 2023

Rating: 9.4

Time: 55.9 Hours

Completion: Main Story

Thoughts:

This was the second souls game I played (after elden ring) but the first I actually completed. I took a step back from Elden Ring (though I enjoyed it) and decided I wanted to appreciate the souls series as a whole, so I took on Dark Souls.

Right off the bat, this game gives me horror vibes. Spawning in a cell, navigating the close quarters of this prison area. Trying to fight these guards one on one while still trying to figure out who and where I am.

And honestly for most of the game, I am at the edge of my seat due to the possibility of enemies popping out of no where and just the overall artistic style of the game, most places seem like towns that were overrun that now are broken down and gloomy. But at the same time this game is graphically beautiful to me, but that might be because it is a remaster, but even then the art direction was spot on and the remaster looks great.

I enjoy the progression of souls games, defeating enemies along a path, slowly testing out and upgrading equipment/builts, and fighting the main boss of an area. It seems pretty simple thinking about it like that but I think it provides some freedom and fun to go at your own pace.

This game is fantastic to me.

Thoughts:

After playing God of War (2018) I decided I wanted to give the whole series a try so I went back to the first.

God of War (2018)

Start Date: April 12th, 2023

End Date: April 27th, 2023

Rating: 9.0

Time: 32.2 Hours

Completion: Main Story

Thoughts:

My first God of War game. And I think it was a great game to start the experience for me.

This game has some beautiful graphics.

I enjoy the sort of slow pace from place to place, with a curious Atreus getting context about Kratos and what is going on in the world.

But after those moments you are solving small problems to progress and slashing any enemies in your way. The combat was pretty simple yet fun, I definitely kept it simple myself as I did not take advantage of most moves that I learned.

I also enjoyed trying to fight the Valkyries upon unlocking them. I think I only did 2 or 3 of them then decided it was enough.

I enjoyed playing this game, mostly only cared to do the main story. But this game inspired me to try the whole series.
